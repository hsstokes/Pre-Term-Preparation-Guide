🖨️ Priority 2 — Printing & Scanning at UAL

You will need to print and scan your homework to bring to class. UAL printers are available on campus and are free to use once you have set up your account.

Where are the printers?
  • IT Open Access areas on campus
  • The Social Space / left of main entrance
  • Two print queues available: Arts Mono (black & white) and Arts Colour

Setting Up Your Print Account

1
Set up printing from your device
▼
  • Go to artsprint.arts.ac.uk
  • Install Mobility Print on your own device
  • Log in with your UAL student credentials
  • Top up your print credit using your UAL ID card
🖨️ UAL Printing Information →
2
Print at a UAL printer
▼
  • Send your document to print from your device or the UAL computers
  • Go to the printer and swipe your UAL ID card
  • Select your print job and collect
3
Scan your A3 design sheets
▼
  • Open the printer lid and place your paper face down on the glass
  • Swipe your UAL ID card on the printer
  • Select Scan → Scan to Email
  • Your scan will be sent to your UAL email address

📸 Photographing instead of scanning

You can photograph 3D experimentations or A3 sheets produced in class — but always at a high resolution (300dpi+) for best quality.

Resolution Guide — Why It Matters

Always scan or photograph your work at the correct resolution. Low resolution makes images blurry and unusable in Photoshop.

💻 Priority 3 — No Laptop? Remote Computer Access

⚠️ Important — read this first

  • It is strongly recommended to bring your own laptop to class
  • Many digital skills lessons are online — having your own device gives you flexibility to work anywhere
  • Remote access works but is slower and less reliable than having Adobe CC installed locally
💻

Best Option

Own laptop with Adobe CC installed — full speed, works online and in class

🖥️

Alternative Option

UAL Remote Desktop — access a UAL computer virtually from any device

How to Access UAL Remote Desktop

📸 UAL Remote Desktop Access
🖥️Add: images/remote-desktop.jpg
UAL Remote Desktop — access Creative Suite virtually from any device.
1
Download VMware Horizon Client
▼
  • Go to the UAL Remote Desktop link below
  • Download the VMware Horizon Client for your device (recommended over the browser version)
  • Install it on your laptop, tablet, or desktop computer
🖥️ Access UAL Remote Desktop →
2
Log in with your UAL credentials
▼
  • Use your UAL username and password
  • Select a UAL computer to connect to
  • You will see a full UAL desktop with all software installed
3
Open Photoshop and work remotely
▼
  • Find Photoshop in the applications menu
  • Open your files — save to UAL OneDrive or cloud storage so you don't lose work
  • Note: performance depends on your internet connection speed

💡 Save your work carefully

Remote desktop sessions can time out. Always save frequently to OneDrive or a cloud folder, not just the remote desktop.
UAL computers have:
  • Full Adobe Creative Suite installed
  • All the same software you would use in class
  • Access from any device — laptop, home computer, even a tablet
