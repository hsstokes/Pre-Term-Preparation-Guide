🖨️ Priority 2 — Printing & Scanning at UAL
Where are the printers? IT Open Access areas and the Social Space (left of main entrance). Two queues: Arts Mono (black & white) and Arts Colour.

How to Print & Scan

1
Set up Mobility Print
▶
  • Go to artsprint.arts.ac.uk
  • Install Mobility Print and log in with your UAL credentials
  • Top up print credit using your UAL ID card
🖨️ UAL Printing Info →
2
Print at a UAL printer
▶
  • Send your document to print from your device or a UAL computer
  • Go to the printer → swipe your UAL ID card → select your job → collect
3
Scan your work
▶
  • Place paper face down on the scanner glass
  • Swipe UAL ID card → Scan → Scan to Email
  • Your scan is sent to your UAL email address

📸 Photographing instead?

Fine for 3D work — but always at 300dpi+ for portfolio quality.
